# GENESIS2PCE This adapter PCB allows connecting Genesis 3 or 6 button controllers to a PC Engine console. It consists of all the components a real PC Engine 6 button controller has + an Atmega328p for interfacing with Genesis controllers. When connecting a Genesis 6 button controller without holding the Mode button, the adapter will go into 6 button mode. This means you can use it as an actual 6 button PC Engine adapter for the handful of games that support it (Street Fighter 2, Art of Fighting, Fatal Fury 2 etc.). To ensure compatiblity with other games and the Sega Genesis 6 button controller, one should hold the Mode button while powering on the adapter to put the controller into 3 button mode and the adapter into 2 button mode. Sega Genesis 3 button controllers will always put the adapter into 2 button mode.
